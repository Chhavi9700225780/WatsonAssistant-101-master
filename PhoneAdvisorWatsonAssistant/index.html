Project Objective

Build an intelligent assistant that recommends phones by understanding user preferences and mapping them to brand/features.

Tools

IBM Cloud, Watson Assistant, intents & entities, dialog tree, GitHub Pages for deployment.

Deliverables

Working chatbot prototype, exported skill JSON, documentation, and presentation slides.

How to use

Open the chat widget (bottom-right) and tell the bot about what you like / dislike in your phone.

1

Greet the bot

Start with "hi" or "hello" — the assistant recognizes greetings and begins the flow.

2

Mention brand or attribute

Try: "I like Apple", "My Galaxy battery is bad", or "Recommend a phone".

3

Get recommendations

The assistant maps your preferences to suitable phone suggestions and follow-up questions.

Testing & Results

Continuous testing improved intent recognition and entity extraction; dialog responses were refined for relevance.
